SANDWICH PANELS

What is sandwich panels?

Sandwich panels are modern, light, durable, heat and sound insulating, safe and environmentally friendly building materials. They consist of an insulating core of polyurethane (PUR), polyisocyanurate (PIR) or mineral wool (MW) and finished with inner and outer layers of galvanized and polyester-coated sheet metal in a range of standard and special colours. For the needs of customers, they offer wall and roof sandwich panels with different thicknesses and lengths depending on the needs of our customers. The panels are used to build cold rooms and various types of warehouse and industrial premises.

CONSTRUCTIONS

Halls are particularly in demand due to the possibility of building large warehouses, which is fast and efficient from the point of view of finance and exploitation. They are characterized by clean shapes and high functionality. If the client wishes, we can use translucent panels, which makes the hall an extremely attractive building. The advantages of the halls made of metal structures are well known. The structures are installed quickly compared to monolithic construction, which in turn saves time and money because they are cheaper. Metal structures are easily disassembled and have one very important quality - mobility. Precisely because of this characteristic, they can be moved to another place if needed.
